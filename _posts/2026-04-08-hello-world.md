Welcome to my blog! I've been meaning to start writing for a while, and today I'm finally doing it.

This space will be a place for me to share thoughts on my research, interesting papers I come across, things I'm learning, and whatever else seems worth putting into words.

Writing is a way of thinking out loud — organizing half-formed ideas into something more coherent, and maybe being useful to someone else along the way. No promises on frequency, but I'll aim to write when I have something worth saying.

Stay tuned.
