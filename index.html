Battery Research

I worked with Professor Venkat Viswanathan and Professor Jay Whitacre on combining robotic experimentation with machine learning to autonomously discover and optimize electrolytes for batteries. In recognition for my work, the Carnegie Mellon Mechanical Engineering Department honored me with the Undergraduate Excellence in Research Award.

Hydrogen Storage Research

I worked with Dr. Michael Whiston on conducting an expert elicitation assessment of the expected future costs and performance of hydrogen storage systems for Fuel Cell Electric Vehicles. I projected hydrogen storage systems' expected development trajectories to guide how to improve hydrogen storage systems' performance to promote the commercialization of FCEVs.

Energy Projects

I worked with other Carnegie Mellon students to collaborate with industry on developing projects applying machine learning to improve the development and deployment of clean energy technology. In one project, we focused on predicting battery cathodes’ capacity based on their properties. In another project, we focused on predicting photovoltaic solar energy production based on historical data and relative weather data.
